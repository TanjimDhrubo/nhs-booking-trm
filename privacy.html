Privacy Policy

GDPR Compliant NHS Standards

Last updated: June 2025 · NHS Booking | TRM

📋 What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data to provide our appointment booking service:

  • Full name and email address
  • Date of birth
  • NHS number (optional)
  • Appointment records and history
  • Check-in timestamps
  • Notification preferences

🎯 Why We Collect It

Your data is collected solely to provide the NHS Booking TRM service including:

  • Creating and managing your appointments
  • Sending appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Maintaining your appointment history
  • Ensuring you receive the correct care at the right time

🔒 How It Is Stored

All data is stored securely using Supabase — a cloud PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure. We implement:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — only you can access your own records
  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Password management via Supabase Auth — passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Regular automated database backups

⚖️ Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object object to processing of your personal data

📅 Data Retention

In line with the NHS Records Management Code of Practice 2021, appointment records are retained for a minimum of 8 years from the date of the last entry. Personal profile data is retained for the duration of your account and deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.

🍪 Cookies & Session Tokens

NHS Booking TRM does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We use:

  • Secure JWT session tokens to keep you signed in during your visit. These tokens are managed automatically by Supabase Auth and expire after your session ends.
  • No third-party analytics. No advertising networks.
  • No data sold to third parties — ever.

📧 Contact Our Data Protection Officer

If you wish to exercise your rights or have a privacy concern, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

Email: dpo@nhsbooking-trm.nhs.uk
Organisation: NHS Booking TRM — Digital Services
Address: Digital Services Division, NHS Booking TRM, London, UK
Response time: Within 30 days as required by UK GDPR
